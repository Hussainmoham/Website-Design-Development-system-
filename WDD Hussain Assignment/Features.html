Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign up for the Premium Hardware Membership?
You can sign up in-store at any register, or online through our website. The membership activation is immediate, so you can start enjoying your benefits right away.
Can I cancel my membership at any time?
Yes, you can cancel your membership at any time. If you cancel before your term is complete, your benefits will remain active until the end of your current billing period.
Is there a limit to how many items I can get delivered for free?
No, there's no limit to the number of free deliveries you can receive as a premium member. However, oversized items may have additional handling fees.
How do I book a consultation with an expert?
You can schedule consultations through our website's member portal, our mobile app, or by calling our dedicated premium member hotline. We recommend booking at least 48 hours in advance.
Do the 10% discounts apply to sale items too?
Yes! Your 10% member discount applies to regular and sale-priced items. The only exceptions are clearance items marked as "final price" and gift cards.
